Product Development:

Fruit flies (D. melanogaster) live approximatly 30-45 days. Their mission is to eat and breed prolifically. This is accomplished anywhere there is a food source. Food prep areas, restaurants, produce departments, bakeries, vending machines, bars, home kitchens, compost bins, and garbage containers.

Many control measures use pesticides, are unsightly, unsanitary, and difficult to use and maintain. These are unacceptable in food handling, preparation, and display areas.

Natural Catch® Fruit Fly traps control fruitfly populations with ease. From packaging to placement, Natural Catch® takes one simple step to activate - eliminating any further cleaning, baiting, or maintenance.
